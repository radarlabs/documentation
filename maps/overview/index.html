Maps Platform

Radar Maps Platform is a cost-effective solution for geocoding APIs, search APIs, routing APIs, and base maps.

Together with our Geofencing Platform, Radar provides full-stack location infrastructure for any product or service. We power location-based experiences for enterprises and startups across hundreds of millions of devices worldwide.

Features#

Geocoding APIs

Forward, reverse, and IP geocoding APIs

Search APIs

Autocomplete, address validation, and place search APIs

Routing APIs

Distance, matrix, and route matching APIs

Maps

A cost-effective alternative to Google Maps and Mapbox
